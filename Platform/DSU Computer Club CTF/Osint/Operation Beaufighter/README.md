On 11 December 1944, Group 604, Royal Australian Air Force (RAAF Pacific), sent a confirmatory memorandum to Headquarters, 1st Tactical Air Force (T.A.F.), RAAF (B.P.S.O. Section). A copy of this document was also forwarded to 77 Wing Headquarters.

The memorandum described an incident involving a Beaufighter aircraft during operations in the South West Pacific during World War II.
